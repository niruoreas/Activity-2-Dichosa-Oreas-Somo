MUSCLE TARGET

Chest

The chest muscle in gym terms is the pectoralis major, a key upper body muscle responsible for movements like pressing and hugging. Building and strengthening this muscle is a common goal in weight training for both strength and appearance.

Back

The back muscle is often referred to as the "dorsal muscles" or simply the "dorsals." This term encompasses the various muscles of the upper and middle back, including the latissimus dorsi, as well as the trapezius, rhomboids, and erector spinae muscles.

Shoulder

The shoulder muscles are also commonly referred to as the "shoulder caps." This term emphasizes the goal of developing well-defined, rounded, and visually appealing shoulders, akin to the appearance of a capped or rounded structure.

Arms

the arm muscles are typically referred to as the "guns" or "pythons." This slang is often used informally to describe well-developed and muscular arms, particularly the biceps and triceps. This makes is a common goal for individuals looking to build upper body aesthetics.

Legs

Strengthening and developing the leg muscle groups is not only crucial for lower body strength but also for overall functional fitness and athletic performance. Well-developed legs provide a solid foundation for various activities.
